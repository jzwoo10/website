Dogs I've Seen

Nice Bus Dog

This is literally the nicest looking dog I have ever seen. I mean in terms of personality.

Purse Puppy

This puppy was spotted yesterday afternoon in the Ferry Building being calmly carried in his owner's crossbody bag.

Bus Chihuahua: The Dichotomy of Dog(?)

  • Castro hippie man:

    This was one of the best things I have seen in SF so far. This man in True Religion jeans and a gray graphic zip-up, like something you would have found in Abercombie & Fitch in the 2010s, dressed like he was 20 but probably in his 60-70s sat down on the seat in front of me with a book and his dog in a sweater. He took out these reading spectacles and read the whole trip, his little white chihuahua in a patterned sweater sitting calmly on his lap. Other people looked and him and couldn't help smiling too. I wanted to take a photo but didn't want to be creepy. Either way the vibes were immaculate.

  • Scared and Agitated:

    This one was the polar opposite. The first time I have seen an agitated, non-calm dog on the Muni. When he/she wasn't looking scared they were growling. I hope they found peace.

3-month-old Corgi

This was the first-ever SF dog I got to pet, and it'll also be the last because contact forms ATTACHMENT. Once I touched that tiny dog we formed a bond.
